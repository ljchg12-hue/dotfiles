# Competitive Ad Analysis Skill

Analyze and benchmark competitor advertising strategies for marketing insights.

## When to Use
- Competitive research
- Ad strategy planning
- Market positioning
- Campaign optimization

## Core Capabilities
- Ad copy analysis
- Creative strategy evaluation
- Channel mix analysis
- Messaging patterns
- Target audience identification
- Performance estimation
- Trend identification

## Analysis Framework
1. **Ad Discovery**: Find competitor ads (Facebook Ad Library, Google Ads Transparency)
2. **Copy Analysis**: Headlines, CTAs, value propositions
3. **Creative Analysis**: Images, videos, formats
4. **Channel Mix**: Platforms used, budget allocation
5. **Performance Indicators**: Engagement, reach estimates
6. **Timeline Analysis**: Campaign duration, frequency

## Tools
- Facebook Ad Library
- Google Ads Transparency Center
- SEMrush
- SpyFu
- Adbeat
- Moat

## Best Practices
- Track ads over time
- Categorize by campaign theme
- Analyze messaging evolution
- Identify successful patterns
- Document learnings
